# Research Brain

Keep `RESEARCH_BRAIN.md` the honest, current single source of truth for the whole opportunity search.

## When to run
- After `/market-census`, `/demand-scan`, `/pain-mine`, or `/opportunity-score`.
- When a new candidate idea appears (add a register row).
- When the user asks "where are we / show me the whole map / what's still open".

## Procedure

1. **Read** the current `RESEARCH_BRAIN.md` and the new finding (the report just produced).

2. **Update the relevant node(s) 1–9:** refresh "Where we stand", add/close items under "Open", link the new evidence doc.

3. **Update the CANDIDATE-IDEA REGISTER:** stage reached + current verdict. **Record kills and reframes, not just progress** — a dead idea with a reason is as valuable as a live one.

4. **Update the DOCUMENT INDEX** if a new report was saved.

5. **Challenge the question.** Add/refresh an item under node 9 if the new finding suggests we're solving the wrong problem or missing a direction. The brain's job includes doubting itself.

6. **Summarize to the user:** what changed, the candidate's new status, and the single most important open question to attack next.

## Output
- Edited `RESEARCH_BRAIN.md` (in place — keep the structure, don't rewrite wholesale).
- A 3–5 line changelog to the user.

## Honesty rules
- Never overwrite a recorded kill to make an idea look alive. The memory must be trustworthy over time.
- Keep node "Where we stand" lines tight (1–3 sentences). Depth lives in the linked evidence docs, not here.
